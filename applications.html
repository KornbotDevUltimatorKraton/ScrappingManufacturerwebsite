Industrial 

Brushless DC drivers for industrial applications are designed to offer a highly efficient, small, and protected solution. Products range from integrated FET drivers for low- to mid-current applications up to smart gate drivers enabling multi-kW motor drive  systems.  In addition, our BLDC portfolio offers integrated control drivers.

  • Efficiency: Low RDS(on) provide great thermal performance and low losses with ultra-low sleep current
  • Small size: our portfolio of smart gate driver products integrate passive components to reduce board size, design complexity and cost with device options to integrate DC/DC regulators, current sense amplifiers, integrated FETs, control and more into a single-chip motor driver solution for smallest size
  • Protection: Fully protected from undervoltage lockout (UVLO), charge pump undervoltage (CPUV), MOSFET overcurrent protection (OCP), gate driver fault (GDF), thermal warning and shutdown (OTW/OTSD) with a fault condition indicator (nFAULT).
